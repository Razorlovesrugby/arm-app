git # Release Notes — v20.1
**29 April 2026**

---

## 🎉 What's New

### Merge Duplicate Players — Clean Up Your Roster
You can now merge duplicate players directly from the Roster page. If two players are actually the same person (e.g. "Jonny Smith" and "Jonathan Smith"), just click the **⋮** menu on the duplicate player card, select **Merge into existing player**, choose the real player, and done. All their availability responses and training attendance move across safely. No data lost, no mess.

> **Simon Archer** — historical caps have been added to his profile.

---

### Player Caps Now Show in Team Sheet Exports
When you export your team sheet via **WhatsApp** or **PDF**, each player's total caps now appears next to their name.

- **Example:** `1. Jonny Wilkinson (91)`
- **Captain example:** `10. Owen Farrell (C) (112)`
- Empty slots still show "Unfilled" as before.

No more cross-referencing the player database — everything you need is right there in the squad list.

---

### Availability Form Improvements
Two handy fixes to keep player profiles up to date:

- **📱 Phone numbers sync automatically** — When a player submits the form with a new phone number, their profile gets updated. This means next time they'll be matched by phone straight away.
- **🎂 Birthday overwrite protection** — If a player already has a real birthday on file, submitting the form won't accidentally overwrite it. Only empty or placeholder birthdays get filled in.

---

### Refreshing the App No Longer Shows a Blank Offline Screen
If you refresh the page or restart the app, you'll now see the app interface instead of a confusing "You're offline" page. Everything just works like it should.
